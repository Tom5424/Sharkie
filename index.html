Movement and Attack

Use the "Arrow Keys" to move Sharkie.

Press "Y" to Shoot Normal Bubbles.

Press "X" to Shoot Poison Bubbles.

Press "Space" to do Fin Slap Attack.

Collectables

Collect Coins to Fill the Progressbar,
if the Progressbar is completely filled up,
you deal 50% more Damage against the Endboss.

Collect the Poison Vessels to Fill the Progressbar
you only have a limited number of Poison Vessels.

Game Description

Shoot Standard Bubble against the Jelly Fish to defeat them, you can only defeat the Jelly Fishes with this Bubble.

Use the Fin Slap Attack to defeat the Puffer Fish, you can only defeat the Puffer Fishes with the Fin Slap Attack.

Shoot Posion Bubble against the Endboss to defeat him, you can only defeat the Endboss with Poison Bubbles.
